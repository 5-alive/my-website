5-Alive Development Community-Based Organization is a nonprofit, community-driven initiative committed to advancing the wellbeing of marginalized populations. Guided by a holistic and rights-based approach, the organization delivers targeted and impactful support to vulnerable groups. Our work focuses on providing mental health assistance to young adults, promoting and protecting the rights of the LGBTQIA+ community, offering care and support to the elderly, empowering adolescent girls and young women (AGYW) through tailored programs, and preventing the spread of HIV through comprehensive education, testing, and sustained community-based support.
