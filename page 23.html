Empowering the LGBTQ+ Community

5 Alive Development Community-Based Organization is a registered community-based organization in Kenya, established in 2020 and officially registered in 2023. It is dedicated to advocating for community development and eradicating poverty, with a focus on education, health services, and improving the lives of infants, preschool children, teenagers, and patients with chronic, non-communicable diseases.

Advocacy & Youth Engagement

  • Tackling Universal Health Coverage: Increasing access to healthcare information on sexual health and HIV prevention to address early pregnancies and reduce infections among youth.
  • Advocacy: Amplifying the voices of young people and advocating for their inclusion in decision-making processes that affect their lives.
  • Economic Empowerment: Equipping youth with practical skills like barbering, salon services, farming, and carpentry to address unemployment and resource access challenges.
  • HIV Prevention and Management: Visiting schools, communities, and churches to promote HIV prevention, abstinence, family planning, and substance abuse awareness among youth.
  • Expanding Initiatives: Continuously exploring new ways to support the youth and marginalized communities in Nakuru and Narok counties.
