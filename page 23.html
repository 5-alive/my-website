Empowering the LGBTQ+ Community

At 5-Alive Development Organization, empowerment isn't just a mission it's a movement. Born from lived experiences and fueled by passion, we are a youth-led, grassroots initiative breaking down barriers to healthcare, dignity, and justice for LGBTQ+ individuals in Kenya. In communities where being LGBTQ+ can mean isolation, discrimination, or worse, 5-Alive creates safe spaces, sparks bold conversations, and delivers life-changing services — from stigma-free HIV care and mental health support to leadership training and economic empowerment.

Why Your Support Matters

Every donation, partnership, and act of solidarity helps us:

  • Reach underserved LGBTQ+ youth with affirming health and psychosocial support.
  • Train peer educators and community leaders to challenge stigma from within.
  • Build bridges between the community and institutions that have long excluded them.
  • Change narratives, influence policy, and protect rights that are too often ignored.

We don’t just respond to crises we build resilience. We don’t just tell stories we rewrite futures.
